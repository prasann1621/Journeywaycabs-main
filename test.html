Mumbai Darshan Details

Discover Mumbai’s iconic landmarks with our Mumbai Sightseeing Tours. Choose from North Mumbai or South Mumbai sightseeing packages and explore the best of the city.

North Mumbai Tour Highlights
  • Worli Sea Face – Scenic view of the Arabian Sea.
  • Nehru Planetarium & Science Centre – Interactive science exhibits.
  • Siddhivinayak Ganpati Temple – Seek blessings of Lord Ganesha.
  • Bandra-Worli Sea Link – An engineering marvel.
  • Bandra Bandstand & Mount Mary Church – Bollywood’s favorite spot.
  • Juhu Beach & Juhu ISKCON Temple – Relax at Mumbai’s famous beach.
South Mumbai Tour Highlights
  • Gateway of India & Taj Mahal Hotel – Mumbai’s heritage landmark.
  • Marine Drive & Girgaon Chowpatty Viewing Deck – The Queen’s Necklace.
  • Kamla Nehru Park & Hanging Gardens – Scenic greenery in the city.
  • Taraporewala Aquarium – India’s oldest aquarium.
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Museum & Flora Fountain – Historical gems.
  • Elephanta Caves & Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST) – UNESCO Heritage sites.
  • Haji Ali Dargah, Mahalaxmi Temple & Mahalaxmi Race Course – Spiritual & elite landmarks.

Lonavala Trip

A Perfect Weekend Getaway!

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city with our Lonavala Trip Package. Enjoy scenic waterfalls, ancient caves, and breathtaking viewpoints on this refreshing journey.

Lonavala Trip Details

Enjoy scenic waterfalls, ancient caves, and breathtaking viewpoints with our Lonavala Trip Package.

Top Attractions Covered
  • Bhushi Dam – A famous monsoon destination with scenic beauty.
  • Tiger Point – Stunning cliffside views, perfect for sunrise/sunset.
  • Karla Caves – Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves.
  • Lonavala Lake – A serene and peaceful getaway.
  • Lonavala Market – Buy famous Chikki & local delicacies.
  • Ryewood Park – A lush green garden for relaxation.
  • Khandala – Another beautiful hill station nearby.
  • Lion’s Point – One of the best viewpoints in Lonavala.
  • Celebrity Wax Museum – Life-like wax statues of celebrities.
  • Shree Narayani Dham Temple – A divine temple with beautiful architecture.
